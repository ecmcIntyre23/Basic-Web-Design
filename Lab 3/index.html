Cities of the World: A Tourist's Dream

Column 1

The city of London is known as the best place to live in the world. It's also nott too shabby in a tourist's perspective. In London, you can visit Big Ben, the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and countless spectacular museums.

Column 2

Paris, France is one of the most attractive and busy tourist destinations in the world. It's home to many scenic places including the Eiffel-Tower.

Column 3

The United States has many notable tourist destinations, but there are not many more visited than New York City. People can see the Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, etc.
